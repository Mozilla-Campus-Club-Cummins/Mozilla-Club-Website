ORIENTATION- Mozilla Campus Club

Mozilla Campus Club CCEW, Pune

October 9th, 2024

Event Overview

Date: October 9th, 2024

Mode: Offline

Venue: K.B.Joshi Auditorium

Starting off our orientation session, we introduced students to the world of open source and got the new semester a great start! We provided a brief overview of the goals of the Mozilla Campus Club, highlighting our varied teams and the innovative projects planned. Curiosity and perceptive inquiries filled the air, demonstrating a strong desire to integrate into the community.

After setting the scene, we shared all the important information for our next recruitment process, providing everyone with a clear way into the team. This event was the ideal beginning to our goal of creating a thriving open-source culture on campus!
