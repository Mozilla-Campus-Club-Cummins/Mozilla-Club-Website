Merge Mania - Mozilla Campus Club

Mozilla Campus Club CCEW, Pune

Date:4th and 5th April 2025

Mode: Offline

Platform: Between IT and Mechanical building

Merge Mania, the Mozilla Campus Club's signature two-day event was presented as part of our college's spectacular annual tech fest, "Innovation". For enthusiasts seeking to immerse themselves in an exciting atmosphere of good times, friendly competition, and teamwork, this event was the perfect gathering place.

Merge Mania challenged students with an exciting lineup of four distinct tech-based games. In 'Technical Atlas', participants tested their knowledge; in 'Guess the Word', they competed against the clock; in 'Googler', they demonstrated their expert search abilities and in our tech-themed '20 Questions', participants solved mysteries.

The event was intended to be a fun introduction to the world of technology and open source. Having a great time together is the best way to ignite interest in technology, as demonstrated by Merge Mania's successful creation of an atmosphere where learning felt like play!
