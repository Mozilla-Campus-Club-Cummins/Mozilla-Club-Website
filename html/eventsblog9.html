Git/Github Workshop - Mozilla Campus Club

Mozilla Campus Club CCEW, Pune

Date: 17th March 2025

Mode: Offline

Platform: Mech Auditorium

The Mozilla Campus Club organized a hand-on workshop on Git and Github to equip students with essential version control skills.

The session began with an introduction to git and github, sharing the importance of these tools, and helped developers build on the basics of open source. Participants learned the fundamentals of repository management, branching and collaboration. The workshop covered key concepts and provided practical experience with a hands-on approach which successfully helped in confidence building. By the end of the workshop students were confident to start collaborating on projects!

The workshop bridged the gap between theoretical knowledge and application and provided a strong foundation for students to contribute effectively and streamline their coding process.With initiatives like this, Mozilla Campus Club CCEW continues to spark curiosity and empower students!
