AI/ML Webinar Report

Mozilla Club - Cummins College of Engineering for Women, Pune

Date: 9th September, 2023

Platform: Zoom Online

Speaker: Abhishek Raj Permani, Machine Learning Engineer, Jaguar Land Rover

Partner: Coding Ninjas

The Mozilla Club of Cummins College of Engineering for Women in Pune conducted a technical webinar on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning on 9th September 2023. The session was held over Zoom and led by Mr. Abhishek Raj Permani, a Machine Learning Engineer at Jaguar Land Rover. He provided enlightening insights into the latest AI and ML technologies.

Key Topics Covered:

  • Supervised and Unsupervised Learning
  • Neural Networks
  • Model Optimization Techniques

Mr. Permani also shared real-world applications of AI, particularly in the automobile industry, such as:

  • Predictive Maintenance
  • AI-based Autonomous Driving Systems
  • Enhancing Customer Experience

The event was organized in collaboration with Coding Ninjas, a premier platform for programming education, which amplified the technical outreach to participants. Students were introduced to industry-standard tools and frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch, helping them get started with machine learning projects.

The webinar concluded with a technical Q&A session where students sought guidance on best practices for AI/ML implementation, career prospects, and overcoming challenges in building AI-powered solutions.

Overall, the event provided both technical knowledge and inspiration, fostering a deeper interest in the field of AI and ML among the participants.
